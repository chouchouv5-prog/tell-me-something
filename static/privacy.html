1. Introduction

Welcome to Tell Me Something ("TMS"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website at tell-me-something.pages.dev.

4. Anonymous Messages

Messages sent through TMS are anonymous — we do not store the identity of message senders. Only the recipient can read their messages.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to keep you logged in and to serve advertisements through Google AdSense. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to our site. You can opt out at google.com/settings/ads.

8. Children's Privacy

TMS is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
