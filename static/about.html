What is Tell Me Something?

The anonymous messaging platform that lets people speak freely and honestly.

🌍 Our Mission

Tell Me Something (TMS) is a free anonymous messaging platform. Create your account, get your unique link, share it on social media, and receive honest anonymous messages from anyone!

We believe in honest communication. Sometimes people have things to say but are afraid to say them publicly. TMS gives everyone a safe space to express themselves freely.

🔒 How we protect your privacy

Every message sent through TMS is completely anonymous. We never store the identity of message senders — not even we know who sent what.

Your data is stored securely and never sold to third parties. You can delete your messages at any time.
